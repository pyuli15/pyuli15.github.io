About Me

I am a Front-End Developer and Game Designer with a BA from New York University in Storytelling Through Video Games from the Gallatin school of Individualized Study. I have always had a passion for all things media and technology growing up, and have focused on implementing my love of video games and tv programming into my career! Some of my favorite games are The Last of Us, Papo & Yo, and What Remains of Edith Finch, to name a few. My goal is to gain the skills to create meaningful experiences in media, that are rich in story, and focus on those who do not get as much attention in the world, this being third world countries and minorities.
