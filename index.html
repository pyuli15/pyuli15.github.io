About Me

I am currently a senior at New York University studying Storytelling Through Video Games at the Gallatin school. I have always had a passion for video games growing up, and decided what better way than to make-them as a career! Some of my favorite games are The Last of Us, Papo & Yo, and What Remains of Edith Finch, to name a few. My goal is to gain the skills to create meaningful gaming experiences that are rich in story, and focus on those who do not get as much attention in the world, this being third world countries and minorities. I am based in New York.
